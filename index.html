Linkage and Collaboration

Maharishi International University
(MIU)

ACT collaborates with MIU to offer postgraduate programs in MBA and MSC in
Computer Science. The preparation to offer PhD in management is also in the
pipeline. MIU is a private University with its main campus located in Fairfield, Iowa,
USA. The University is accredited through the PhD level by the Higher Learning
Commission of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools
(www.ncacihe.org).
